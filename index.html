My Projects

Water Dispenser with Object Detection

Introducing a water dispenser with object detection represents a significant advancement in both convenience and efficiency, merging cutting-edge technology with everyday utility. By integrating object detection capabilities into traditional water dispensers, this innovative solution revolutionizes the user experience, offering a seamless and hygienic way to access water.

Gone are the days of manual interaction with handles or buttons, which can harbor germs and lead to hygiene concerns. With object detection technology, users simply approach the dispenser, and sensors detect the presence of their container. This touch less operation not only enhances hygiene by minimizing physical contact but also streamlines the dispensing process, making it more intuitive and user-friendly.

Furthermore, water dispensers equipped with object detection are designed to be smart and efficient. It can accurately detect the cup in the field of vision and spills water into the cup.
